Welcome to LUGOD
The Linux Users' Group of Davis

We hold meetings twice a month to support both the social and technical needs of Linux users.
We have a social meeting the first Tuesday of ever month (generally at Crepeville on 3rd Street in Davis. The next social meeting is 7pm on Tuesday 3rd of April, at Crepeville in Davis.

Our general meeting is usually a demonstration or discussion of something related to Linux or FLOSS, and is normally the third Monday each month.
The next general meeting is Monday, 16th of April. The topic and location have yet to be announced.
